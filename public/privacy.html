Privacy Policy

Curated — London Venue Discovery

What we collect

When you use Curated, we may collect the following data:

  • Your email address (used for sign-in)
  • Venues you save to your collection
  • Connections you make with friends on the platform
  • Basic usage analytics (screens viewed, features used)

How we use it

We use your data to:

  • Personalise your venue recommendations and itineraries
  • Power AI-generated day and night plans tailored to your preferences
  • Enable sharing itineraries and saved spots with friends you connect with
  • Improve the app experience based on aggregate usage patterns

Third parties

We use the following third-party services to operate Curated:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (stores your account and saved venues)
  • Google Places API — venue data, ratings, and location information
  • Anthropic Claude — AI-powered itinerary generation and venue parsing

We do not sell your data to any third party. Data shared with the services above is limited to what is necessary for the app to function.

Data retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account exists. When you delete your account, all associated data (saved venues, connections, analytics) is permanently removed from our systems.

Your rights

You can exercise the following rights at any time via the Me screen in the app:

  • Export — download all your saved data
  • Delete — permanently delete your account and all associated data

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or your data, contact us at privacy@curated.london.
